Chaos;Head NoAH

Chaos;Head NoAH is a psychological thriller visual novel developed by 5pb. and Nitroplus. It follows Takumi Nishijo, a high school student who becomes entangled in a series of bizarre and gruesome murders known as the "New Generation Madness." The story explores themes of delusion, reality, and perception, making it one of my favourite visual novels of all time.
